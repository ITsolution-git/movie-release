About Current Movie Releases

Current Movie Releases is a progressive web application about movies.

We provide the latest information for the now playing and upcoming movies. Theatrical, high quality trailers, movie overviews, actors and other celebrities, directors, producers with the entire cast and crew list. You will also find some interesting technical film details. The movies are reviewed by some of our best film critics and rated by our best users. :)

Feel free to browse our movie collections and submit your rating about the preferred "Must See". Every user's rating counts as a voter on the movie. If you have a favorite, you can add that to your Favorites list, find a great film, you can always recommend it to your friends via the built in social buttons. A variety of social networking tools are available just for our users who plan explore the movie universe. We want you to share and write your own opinion too, because we all can determine if a movie is worth watching or not!
The highest rated films will be added daily to our "Best of Releases" lists, so you'll the most recent, best rated ones. If you want to receive instant notifications you are welcome install the CMR web application in your Google Chrome browser, works in desktop and on Android too. We send out news and interesting TOP of posts to our social networks Facebook, Twitter, Google+, Digg and Reddit. Feel free to join us!
