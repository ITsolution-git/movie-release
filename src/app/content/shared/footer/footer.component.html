About Current Movie Releases

Current Movie Releases is a progressive web application about movies for movie lovers.

We are happy to be your go-to site for all the information surrounding the newest film releases. Our huge database covers everything from classics to the best movies now playing in theaters, and it's always growing! Whether it's a movie that's now playing in theaters or an upcoming movie, we'll have all the details about your most anticipated films! We offer everything from HD trailers to full cast and crew lists. Each movie page will tell you the director, producer, interesting trivia, and even reviews written by some of the best critics in the business. Users can also rate movies themselves, forming a community rating that will let you know what our audience thinks is worth watching.

We are big proponents of listening to our users, so be sure to browse our extensive list of thousands of movies. Mark your favorite films as "Must See", so other users will find them quicker! As you browse, you can form a list of your favorite films and share it with your friends, thanks to the wide array of built-in social media sharing options. We truly care about your opinion on current movie releases and oldies, so we encourage you to write your own reviews and ratings so other users know what's worthwhile!

The films that are rated the best will pop up in the "Best of Releases" category, so if you're looking for a solid movie to watch, be sure to check that first! If you want up-to-date notifications from our site, feel free to install the Current Movie Release web application! It functions perfectly with Google Chrome, and can be used both on desktops and Android mobile phones. If you're a fan of social media, be sure to follow us on all of our social media platforms for great posts relating to new movies and industry buzz.

Take some time and explore the web application, and let us know what you think are the best movies of all time!
